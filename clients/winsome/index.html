What BTT Is

Three things. One powerful system.

BTT was built around three outcomes — for your clients, for your family, and for your future. Everything we do runs through these three pillars.

01
🛡️
Protection

You help families get protected. Life insurance provides financial security that most families desperately need and often don't know how to access. You become the person who makes that happen.

02
💳
Debt Freedom

Many of the families you work with are looking for a way out of debt. The products and strategies you'll learn to present give them a real path — and you earn while helping them get there.

03
📈
Income Growth

Whether someone wants extra income or wants to build their own agency, BTT gives them a vehicle. And when you recruit others into this system, you earn on their production too — real residual income.

The Process

Four steps from applying to earning.

No guesswork. Clear path. Here's exactly what happens once you reach out.

1
Apply & Attend a BOM

Fill out the form and a Marketing Director contacts you within 24 hours. If there's a fit, you'll receive a personal invite to our next Business Opportunity Meeting (BOM) at our Pompano Beach office. This is a one-time invite. Come see what we do, ask every question you have, and decide if this is right for you. We don't pressure anyone twice.

2
Get Licensed & Appointed

We guide you through your state life insurance license (1–2 weeks, fast track) and then your carrier appointment. Your team is with you every step — we've done this with dozens of new agents.

3
Field Training — We Go With You

This is where BTT is different. We don't hand you a packet and say "good luck." We field-train you — sitting alongside you in appointments, helping you present, handling objections — until you're ready to run independently.

4
Build Your Business

Once you're moving, you can grow solo or build a team. Either way, the AI system handles follow-ups and your pipeline stays warm. Part-time agents let their team work leads while they're learning — and still earn.

Who This Is For

You don't need a sales background.

BTT works for people across every stage of life. Here's who thrives in this system — and why.

⏱️
Working a Job Right Now?

Start part-time. Our AI system keeps your leads warm between your working hours. Your team covers appointments until you're ready to step in yourself. Many BTT agents built this alongside a full-time job.

🤝
You Don't Like "Selling"?

Good — because this isn't really selling. You're having conversations about things families already need. The system does the follow-up. Your team handles the close until you're confident. You just need to care about people.

🚀
Ready to Go All In?

If you want to build fast, BTT gives you everything you need to run. The system, the training, the team, and the tools are all in place. The ceiling is yours to set.

🌱
Completely New to This Industry?

Zero experience is perfectly fine. We've built agents from scratch through field training and the BOM system. You bring the willingness — we bring the path. That's the BTT model.
